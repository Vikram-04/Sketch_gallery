    • About Me

    Hey there! I’m Vikram — a curious mind, a computer science student, and an aspiring software developer. Ever since I was young, I’ve been fascinated by how machines, which aren’t alive, can "think" like us — solve problems, make decisions, and run entire systems. Learning about the binary number system, transistors, and logic gates felt like discovering magic hidden in circuits. That wonder pulled me into pursuing a degree in computer science, where I’m diving deeper into the world of programming, systems, and all things tech (even the not-so-interesting bits!).

    Beyond code, I have a creative side that loves to escape into stories. Movies and shows let me disconnect from the real world and connect with characters whose journeys stick with me long after the credits roll. That emotional connection sparked a new passion: sketching portraits of the characters I love. There's something special about turning pixels on a screen into pencil strokes on paper — it’s like giving their stories a second life.

    This website is my little corner of the internet, where I share these portraits and the passion behind them. Thanks for stopping by!
